"Art at GVSU” is your window for discovering, exploring, and sharing artwork from the Grand Valley State University’s art collection. 
 
Browse art from every building on our 5 Michigan campuses (Allendale, Grand Rapids, Muskegon, Holland, and Traverse  City). 
 
Learn more about an artwork you see on campus by looking it up.
 
Discover new favorites by viewing our featured art tours. 
 
Highlights include:
* geo-locations for artwork
* customizable favorite list
* full social-media integration
 
Notes:
WiFi recommended
